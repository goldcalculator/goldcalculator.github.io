Gold Valuation Guide

Calculating the value of gold involves three key factors: Weight, Purity (Karat), and the current Market Price.

1. Understanding Purity

24K (99.9%): Investment-grade pure gold.
22K (91.6%): Standard for Indian jewelry.
18K (75.0%): Used for diamond jewelry.
14K (58.3%): Durable and affordable everyday wear.

2. Calculation Formula

The Price is calculated using the following logical formula:

Value = (Weight × Market Price) × (Karat / 24)

3. Fees and Taxes

  • Making Charges: Usually 5% to 20% depending on the design.
  • GST/Taxes: Don't forget to add local sales tax (like 3% GST in India).
  • Hallmarking: Always look for the BIS hallmark for authenticity.
